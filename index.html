About Tim Triemstra

Work at Apple as an engineering manager on developer tools, documentation, and Swift open source. Have worked at Apple since 2005, originally as the product marketing lead for Xcode, UNIX, compilers, and Swift for the first 16 years.

Currently live in San Diego with my wife, Maggie, and young daughter, Luana. Attended the University of Michigan in the early 1990s, and before that Detroit Renaissance High School.

Originally taught myself BASIC and then 6502 assembly language on a Commodore 64. Won my first PC/DOS computer at age fifteen while representing Detroit Public Schools in a national programming competition.

Never owned an Apple computer before joining the company. However, after college I worked at a food export company maintaining a custom NeXT-based trading application built with Objective-C and Interface Builder.

Fun fact: the langauge Swift was first announced publicly on my birthday.
